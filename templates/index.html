About the Project

Hello-NYC is a web application that helps people start and grow their businesses in New York City. It is a platform that allows users to research their business ideas, add tasks, and receive feedback from a chatbot. The chatbot will help users with their business ideas and provide them with resources to help them grow their business.

Over 60% of first-time entrepreneurs in NYC feel unprepared to tackle business planning and management challenges.

NYC is known for its intricate licensing, zoning, and compliance requirements. Opening a restaurant in NYC requires up to 15 permits and licenses from multiple city departments.

Entrepreneurs face significant financial hurdles due to NYC's high costs of living, rent, and labor. The average cost of starting a small business in NYC is $50,000–$75,000.

20% of new businesses fail in the first year, and 50% fail within five years. A lack of proper guidance and resources is a leading cause.

Around 72% of entrepreneurs prefer self-service digital tools and online platforms for research, networking, and task management over traditional in-person workshops.

New York City enforces over 6,000 rules and regulations, along with approximately 250 business-related licenses and permits. Navigating this complex regulatory environment can be daunting for new business owners.

Our Data

Whether you're navigating regulations or seeking inspiration from startup success stories...
